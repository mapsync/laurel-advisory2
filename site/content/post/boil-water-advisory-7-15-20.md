A boil water advisory has been issued for the following area due to line maintenance. From the intersection of Lily School Rd and U.S 25 to intersection of U.S 25 and Robinson Creek Rd to include Slate Ridge Rd, Echo Valley Rd , Lily School Rd and Robinson Creek Rd to include all side Rds.  Bring water to a rolling boil before consuming.
