The boil water advisory that had been issued for the following area due to a line break has been lifted as of 7/11/2024 at 10:13am. From 3759 Hwy 552 to the intersection of Sepulvada Blvd and Hwy 552 to include Line Creek, Grace Loop, all of Barker/Tackett subdivision to include all side roads.
