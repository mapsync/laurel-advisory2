A boil water advisory has been issued for the following area. From the intersection of American Greeting road and North Highway 1223 to the intersection of North Highway 1223 and Springfield Drive to include Springfield subdivison and all side roads. Bring water to a rolling boil before consuming. Questions regarding this advisory call our office at 606-878-2494.
