A boil water advisory has been issued for the following area due to valve maintenance. From the intersection of Sublimity School Rd and Hwy 1006 to the intersection of Hwy 1006 and Watermelon Ln to include all side roads and Cross Keys Subdivision. Bring water to a rolling boil before consuming.
