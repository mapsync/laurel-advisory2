A boil water advisory has been issued for the following area due to a line break. From the intersection of South Highway 25 and Highway 2392 to the intersection of Highway 2392 and Horsecreek to include all side roads. Bring water to a rolling boil before consuming. Questions regarding this advisory contact our office at 606-878-2494.
