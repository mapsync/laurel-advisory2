A boil water advisory has been issued for the following area due to a line break. From 3759 Hwy 552 to the intersection of Sepulvada Blvd and Hwy 552 to include Line Creek, Grace Loop, all of Barker/Tackett subdivision to include all side roads. Bring water to a rolling boil before consuming. Questions regarding this advisory contact our office at 606-878-2494.
