A boil water advisory has been issued for the following area due to a line break. From the intersection of Hwy 552 and Sepulvada Blvd to 3785 Hwy 552 to include all of Tackett Subdivision, Line Creek, Grace Loop , Fox den , Crystal Ln to include all side roads. Bring water to a rolling boil before consuming. Questions regarding this advisory contact our office at 606-878-2494.
