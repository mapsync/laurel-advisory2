From the Intersection of Mcgill Pine and Philpot road to the Intersection of Philpot road and 363 and also from the Intersection of Mcgill Pine and Philpot road to the Intersection of Mcgill Pine and 1006 to include all side roads. Bring water to a rolling boil before consuming. Questions regarding this advisory call our office at 606-878-2494.
