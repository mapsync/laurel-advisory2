The boil water advisory that had been issued for the following area due to line maintenance has been lifted as of 7-16-20 at 9:30 am. From the intersection of Lily School Rd and U.S 25 to intersection of U.S 25 and Robinson Creek Rd to include Slate Ridge Rd, Echo Valley Rd , Lily School Rd and Robinson Creek Rd to include all side Rds.
