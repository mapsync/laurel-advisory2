A boil water advisory has been issued for the following area. From the intersection of Powers road and American Greeting Card road to the intersection of North Hills and American Greeting Card road to include Cumberland River Comp Care. Bring water to a rolling boil before consuming. Questions regarding this advisory call our office at 606-878-2494.
